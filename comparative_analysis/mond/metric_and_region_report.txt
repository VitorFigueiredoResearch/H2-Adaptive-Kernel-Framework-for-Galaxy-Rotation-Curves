1. HARMONIZED SCATTER METRIC
------------------------------
Metric: RMS of log10(V_model) - log10(V_obs) [dex]

  sigma = sqrt( mean( [log10(V_tot(R_i)) - log10(V_obs(R_i))]^2 ) )

Source: Directly from H2 operational diagnostic (diagnostics/test3_inner_scatter.py)
        and confirmed as the metric used in the NFW comparative analysis.

For the MOND/RAR comparison:
  V_tot(r) = sqrt(g_obs(r) * r)  where g_obs is from the RAR formula
