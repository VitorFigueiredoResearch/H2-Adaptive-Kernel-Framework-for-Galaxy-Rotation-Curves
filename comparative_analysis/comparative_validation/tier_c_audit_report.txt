6. ARCHIVAL GAP CAUSE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The gap is structural, not random:
  - fleet_expansion_30galaxy/ was published with full phase3 + phase4 outputs
  - fleet_expansion_50galaxy/ and fleet_expansion_80galaxy/ were published
    with fleet summary CSVs and figures only — phase4 per-galaxy outputs
    were not included.

This means 44/80 fleet galaxies (55%) have no archived log10-dex
phase4 output. The fleet summary records delta_sigma_kms = 0.0 for all
of them (stop rule never triggered), but the log10-dex sigma_H2 metric
cannot be computed without the phase4 CSV or a working phase4 rerun.

Q: Are any Tier C galaxies inner-coverage N/A cases?

Q: What are the 44 Tier C galaxies?
A: Valid H2 fleet members with confirmed fleet null result
   (delta_sigma_kms = 0.0) but no archived log10-dex phase4 output.
   They are "no_archive" cases, not "measurement failure" cases.

Q: Can the 44 be recovered without rerunning the H1 basis pipeline?
A: NO. Phase4 requires H1 basis runs at multiple L values, and the
   H1 basis pipeline currently fails at L=10 kpc for all tested
   galaxies (NGC3198, NGC3521). Recovery would require either:
   (a) fixing the H1 basis computation at L=10 kpc, or
   (b) modifying phase4 BasisConfig to exclude L=10 from the grid.
   Neither is within the scope of the current comparative_validation task.
